# 5-Day AI Agents Intensive Course with Google

## Course Overview

- **Start Date**: Sunday, November 9, 2025
- **Duration**: 5 Days
- **Platform**: Kaggle
- **Live Sessions**: Daily at 11 AM PT / 8 PM CET / 12:30 AM IST (starting Monday, Nov 10)
- **Hosts**: Kanchana Patlolla and Anant Nawalgaria

## Course Structure

### Daily Schedule

1. **Content Release**: New assignments posted daily on [Kaggle course page](https://www.kaggle.com)
2. **Materials**: Whitepapers, codelabs, and podcast episodes
3. **Live Sessions**: YouTube livestreams with Google researchers and special guests (NVIDIA, Cohere, etc.)
4. **Recordings**: Available on Discord after each session

### Capstone Project (Optional)

- Complete on the final day
- Apply learned concepts by building your own AI agent
- Earn a Kaggle badge upon completion
